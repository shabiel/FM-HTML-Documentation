Preface

The "VA FileMan Getting Started Manual" is designed to provide you, the VistA user, with "how to" information on the functions of VA FileMan, VistA's database management system. The features of VA FileMan are used throughout in most VistA applications.

This manual shows how to use the features of VA FileMan that are likely to be used by all VistA users. It introduces basic VA FileMan concepts, and shows you how to use VA FileMan's basic tools for displaying and editing data.
